    Cycle is basically Tron, though I've added my own stuff to it.  For example, if one of the
snakes runs into its own trail, the whole trail disappears.  In order to win the game, you have to
get your opponent to run into your trail.

Controls, Player One (Red Snake)
   W - Move Up
   A - Move Left
   S - Move Down
   D - Move Right

Controls, Player Two (Green Snake)
   I - Move Up
   J - Move Left
   K - Move Down
   L - Move Right
